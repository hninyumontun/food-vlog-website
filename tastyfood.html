Spaghetti Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 12 ounces (340g) spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 slices of bacon or pancetta, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish (optional)
Cooking Steps:
  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti and set it aside.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese in a bowl. Season with black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the diced bacon or pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set it aside on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease.
  4. In the same skillet with the bacon drippings, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, then add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic and bacon drippings. Toss well to coat the spaghetti.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat, then quickly pour the egg and Parmesan mixture over the spaghetti while continuously tossing the spaghetti to coat it evenly. The residual heat from the spaghetti will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  7. Add the cooked bacon back to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined.
  8. Season the spaghetti carbonara with salt and additional black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
  9. Serve the spaghetti carbonara immediately, topped with extra grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
